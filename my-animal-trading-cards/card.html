American Crow

It's believed that the American Crow can remember specific humans that they meet.

  • Scientific Name: Corvus brachyrhynchos
  • Average Length: 40–53 cm (16–21 in)
  • Average Lifespan: 7–8 years
  • Habitat: Arboreal in virtually all types of country

The American crow is a large bird with iridescent black feathers all over. The typical wingspan for an adult is 85-100 cm (33 to 39 in). Their most usual call is a loud, and rapid "caaw-caaw-caaw."
